Below is a full list of programmes offered at ICA (MCAST Mosta Campus), from foundation to degree level.

Introductory Certificate in the Creative Arts

MQF Level 1

Introduces basic skills in drawing, visual storytelling, and digital tools. For students needing support before progressing to Level 2.

Foundation Certificate in Creative Arts

MQF Level 2

Basic introduction to various creative fields including drawing, media, and design with practical assignments.

Diploma in Art and Design

MQF Level 3

Builds fundamental skills in traditional and digital design for learners preparing for advanced diplomas.

Advanced Diploma in Art and Design

MQF Level 4

Focus on visual communication, illustration, sculpture, and experimental design practices. Portfolio building for higher education.

Advanced Diploma in Video Production

MQF Level 4

Study filmmaking, audio editing, media planning, and journalism using industry-standard software and workflows.

Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design

MQF Level 4

Explore layout, branding, digital illustration, packaging, and typography. Strong emphasis on industry portfolio projects.

Advanced Diploma in Photography

MQF Level 4

Master DSLR and studio photography, lighting setups, editing, and exhibition techniques.

Advanced Diploma in Fashion and Retail

MQF Level 4

Combines creative clothing design with retail business, merchandising, and fashion marketing fundamentals.

Advanced Diploma in Performing Arts

MQF Level 4

Training in acting, voice, dance, stagecraft, and theatre production, preparing for performance or drama degrees.

Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Fine Art

MQF Level 6

Degree-level study in contemporary art practice, history, theory, and professional studio work. Includes exhibitions and critiques.

Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Graphic Design

MQF Level 6

Advanced skills in digital, web, motion, and print design. Emphasis on portfolio development and industry partnerships.

Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Photography

MQF Level 6

Professional photographic techniques, critical thinking, exhibition curation, and freelance/industry preparation.

Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Performing Arts

MQF Level 6

Advanced exploration of performance theory, practice, directing, and devising in live and digital contexts.
